Introduction

We are a student-driven platform that aims to help connect learners through study groups and peer tutoring.

Our history

This platform started as an idea between some friends who helped supported eachother during exam periods by studying together in groups.

It started small, with fewer than ten students. Over time, more students started reaching out for support, and the group gradually expanded.

At first, our study sessions were held online on various social media platforms. However, as the more people showed up, we decided to start looking for a dedicate space to organise more activities.

The city library responded to our request and helped us find a suitable location. Teachers from several schools and universities also joined and provided support and funding.

Eventually, we found a perfect place where our program could grow intro a structured learning community.

Our mission

From the start, our goal has been to make learning more accessible, engaging and effective for every student.

The whole idea behind was to support students who struggle to keep up with their courses by providing a collaborative and supportive learning environment.

We believe that students often learn better from their peers, which is why we focus on encouraging peer-to-peer learning support.

Our long-term vision is to inspire more people to create similar study hubs all over the country, because we believe that collaboration is the key to academic success.

What makes us different?

Student driven

Flexible
